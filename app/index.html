About Us

LAV Productions, LLC is a company that specializes in labor for the convention and corporate meeting industry. With a hands on approach, the senior members of LAV have managed to hand pick some of the best technicians in the country. We feel that this approach is the best way to ensure that our clients have a great, seamless show.As a result this way of thinking has had a direct impact to the overall success of our clients.
